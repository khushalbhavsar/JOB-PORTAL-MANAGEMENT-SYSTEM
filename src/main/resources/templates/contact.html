Frequently Asked Questions

Click on the "Register" button in the navigation bar, fill in your details, and select your role (Job Seeker or Recruiter). You'll receive a confirmation and can start using the platform immediately.

Browse jobs using the search filters, click on a job you're interested in, and click the "Apply Now" button. You can add a cover letter and your application will be sent to the recruiter.

Job Portal is completely free for job seekers. Recruiters can post jobs and access candidates without any charges. We believe in making job opportunities accessible to everyone.

Log in to your account and go to your dashboard. You'll see all your applications with their current status - Applied, Under Review, Shortlisted, Interview Scheduled, or Hired.
